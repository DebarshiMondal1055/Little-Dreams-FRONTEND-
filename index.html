About Us

At Little Dream Films, we believe every family’s journey is filled with moments worth remembering. From the soft glow of maternity to the joyful excitement of birthdays, we are dedicated to capturing each chapter with authenticity, warmth, and artistic storytelling.

We specialise in Maternity Photography, Baby Shower Photography, Newborn Photography, Naming Ceremony Photography, Birthday Photography, and Family Portrait Photography — offering a complete photography experience for growing families. Each session is designed to feel natural, comfortable, and personal, allowing you to relax while we preserve your most meaningful memories.

Our work focuses on real emotions — the gentle smiles, the tiny details, the expressions that make your family unique. Whether you’re welcoming a new life, celebrating a milestone, or creating portraits together, we ensure every frame reflects love, connection, and timeless beauty

With creative vision and thoughtful guidance, our team crafts images that feel genuine and elegant. We take the time to understand your story and style, bringing out the essence of your family in every photograph.

Little Dream Films is built on trust, passion, and the belief that memories are the heart of every family.

From bump to baby and beyond — we’re here to capture every beautiful moment along the way.
